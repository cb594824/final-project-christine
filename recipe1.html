Looking for a healthy alternative to your typical burrito bowl from Chipotle? Well, this might be the perfect solution! Here's what you'll need to make Stuffed Peppers

  • Ingredients:
  • 4 Medium or Large Peppers
  • 1 cup of brown or white rice
  • 1 cup of corn
  • 1 cup of sauteed veggies
  • 1 cup of black or pinto beans
  • 1 cup of shredded lettuce
  • 1 lb of ground chicken
  • 1 cup of grated cheese

Steps:

Preheat the oven to 365 degrees

Step 1: Start preparing your dish by browning the ground chicken

Step 2: Boil the rice and let it cool after cooking

Step 3: Sautee the vegetables in a frying pan

Step 4: Cut the top off of each pepper

Step 5: Hollow out the middle of the peppers by cutting against the inside of each; slicing the stems which connect to the sides of the peppers

Step 6: Fill the peppers with a combination rice, beans, ground chicken and lettuce

Step 7: Sprinkle the tops of the peppers with cheeese and put them in the oven

Step 8: Bake the peppers in the oven until the cheese is crispy
