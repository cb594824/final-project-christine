What could be better than a bowl of hot soup on a cold winter day? With the holidays approaching, a comforting bowl of soup may be all you need to recharge and continue with your shopping!

    Ingredients:
  • 12 Ounces of Chicken Broth
  • 1 Package of Frozen Tortellini
  • 1 Large Chopped Carrot
  • 1 Chopped Celery Stalk
  • 1/2 Chopped Onion
  • 1 Clove of Minced Garlic
      • Steps:
      • In a large sauce pan, brown the chopped onion and minced garlic
      • After the onions and garlic are browned, add the chicken broth and let it sit until it boils
      • Once the broth is ready, add the carrots and celery to the sauce pan
      • The final step is to add the Totellini to the mix
      • The soup is ready to serve when the tortellini rises to the top and is to your liking
