What could be better than a bowl of hot soup on a cold winter day? With the holidays approaching, a comforting bowl of soup may be all you need to recharge and continue with your shopping!

    Ingredients:
  • 12 Ounces of Chicken Broth
  • 1 Package of Frozen Tortellini
  • 1 Large Chopped Carrot
  • 1 Chopped Celery Stalk
  • 1/2 Chopped Onion
  • 1 Clove of Minced Garlic
      • Steps:

    Step 1: In a large sauce pan, brown the chopped onion and minced garlic

    Step 2: After the onions and garlic are browned, add the chicken broth and let it sit until it boils

    Step 3: Once the broth is ready, add the carrots and celery to the sauce pan

    Step 4: The final step is to add the Totellini to the mix

    Step 5: The soup is ready to serve when the tortellini rises to the top and is to your liking
