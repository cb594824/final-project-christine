Chocolate and peanutbutter, need I say more? Oh wait, this sweet and salty item can actually be made healthier, in only three ingredients, than the store-bought cup little piece of Heavan wrapped in foil.

    Ingredients:
  • 2 Tbsp. PB2 (powdered peanutbutter)
  • 1 bag of dark chocolate morsels
  • 1 Tbsp. of Water

    Steps:

    Step 1: Fill the bottom of a double boiler with water and let it come to a boil

    Step 2: Make the peanutbutter by combining the 2Tbsp. of PB2 with 1 Tbsp. of water and mix them together

    Step 3:Once the water has come to a boil, add the chocolate morsels to the top part of the double boiler

    Step 4:Make sure you are stirring the chocolate so it does not burn!

    Step 5:Grease the pan

    Step 6:Fill the cupcake pan half way with chocolate

    Step 7:Add a scoop of peanutbutter to each cup

    Step 8:Cover the peanutbutter with the remaining chocolate and enjoy!
