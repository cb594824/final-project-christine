Not enough time in the morning to make a full-out breakfast feast? Here's a healthy alternative to a quick and delicious breakfast! Even better? You can take this Fresh Fruit Smoothie on the go!

    Ingredients:
  • 1 medium banana
  • 1 small fuji apple
  • 3 fresh strawberries
  • 1 small kiwi
  • 1 cup water

    • Steps:

    Step 1: The night before, freeze your banana to chill your smoothie

    Step 2: Cut up your fruit before putting it in the blender

    Step 3: Combine fruit in the blender

    Step 4: Add the cup of water or enough to cover the fruit 3/4 of the way

    Step 5: Place the top on the blender and blend until desired thickness is achieved

    Step 6: Pour smoothie in a glass and enjoy!
