Finally, a guide to cooking in college that is actually affordable. This isn't recommended for those attempting to maintain a Caveman diet, No Carb, All Carb, or any other sort of specific diet. Welcome to a world of "I'm Hungery, Let's Eat!". Here you will find a wide variety of recipes all for all occassions.

As a Senior in college, I understand the importance of saving time. However, I also understand the importance of eating healthy and living on a tight budget. If we wanted, we could blow our money on McDonalds, or eating out constantly, however, that is not the healthy choice! Come join me on my journey towards eating healthy and let's explore a variety of new recipes together.

About the Author: My names Christine and I am a senior in college. I have always loved to cook and am excited to continue my cooking journey!
